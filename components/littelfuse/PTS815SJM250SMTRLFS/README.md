# PTS815SJM250SMTRLFS

Littelfuse/C&K PTS815 top-actuated SMT momentary switch, exact M-force,
2.5 mm-height, tape-and-reel variant.

![Schematic symbol preview](symbol.svg)

![Four-land footprint preview](footprint.svg)

The important detail is the four-terminal topology: terminals 1 and 2 are
internally common, and terminals 3 and 4 are internally common. The switch
closes between those two poles when actuated.

Status: cross-checked source record; no physical continuity, assembly, or fit
test. The SVGs are inspection previews, not EDA or manufacturing files.
